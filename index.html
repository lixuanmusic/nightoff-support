About NightOff

Every night, you negotiate with yourself. One more video. Five more minutes. It is a negotiation you lose every day. NightOff ends that cycle.

NightOff is a screen blocker for your Mac. It does not use health warnings, productivity tracking, or gamified rewards. It simply stops the late-night inner friction by blocking your screen at the scheduled time.

Key Features

Firm Block

The app blocks access to your screen to break the browsing habit and end the day's friction.

Mindful Delays

If you must delay the rest, NightOff ensures you make that choice consciously, not on autopilot.

Peaceful Rest

A warm, judgment-free interface that helps you put the day to rest without guilt.
